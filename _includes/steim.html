STEIM

STEIM (the STudio for Electro-Instrumental Music) is an independent electronic music center unique in its dedication to live performance. The foundation’s artistic and technical departments support an international community of performers, musicians, and visual artists, to develop unique instruments for their work.

Further information on the STEIM website .

Address: Achtergracht 19, Amsterdam.

From Amsterdam Central Station you can:

Take tram 4 (which you will find in front of the main exit), a ticket (ca €3) can be purchased on the tram. Disembark at the stop ‘Frederiksplein’. Walk back and turn right at the traffic lights. You will pass the Albert Heijn supermarket (note: you will also pass Frederiksplein number 19 – this is NOT STEIM); Keep walking until you see the houseboats, there is the STEIM office at Achtergracht 19.

A taxi from Central station to STEIM office will be around € 14, you can find them on the right side when you exit the station.
